Not branded prompts
Your job is to generate not branded prompts for all the following topics:
* Topic = TOFU Product:
* Topic = BOFU Product:
* Topic = TOFU Product + ICP (one line for each ICP the company markets to)
* Topic = BOFU Product + ICP (one line for each ICP the company markets to)
* Topic = TOFU Product + attributes (one line for each product attribute the company want to feature)
* Topic = BOFU Product + attributes (one line for each product attribute the company want to feature)
* Topic= TOFU Product + location (if it makes sense. One line for each location the company wants to market to)
* Topic= BOFU Product + location (if it makes sense. One line for each location the company wants to market to)

For each topic whenever you generate prompts, you should:
* Generate 2 prompts for TOFU topic
* Generate 5 prompts for BOFU topics

The output should follow this format:
"Prompt", "topic", country (2 letter code), tag0;tag1;tag2;tag3;etc 
Where: 
prompt = the generated prompt 
topic = "product"-"ICP"-"attribute"-"funnel stage" (combined with hyphens, no quotes around the terms
Country is a two letter code of the country in lowercase 
tag0 = product 
tag1 = ICP (if any) 
tag2 = product attribute (if any) 
tag3 = location(if any) 
tag4 = funnel stage (TOFU or BOFU)
